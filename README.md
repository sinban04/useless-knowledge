# Useless Knowledge

**Live at:** <https://sinban04.github.io/useless-knowledge/>

A place to share the "useless knowledge" we have studied — purely out of curiosity, with no career or productivity goal attached. The label is self-deprecating; the knowledge itself is real.

## About

This repo is the public-sharing counterpart of [`probe-lab`](https://github.com/sinban04/useless-engineers/tree/main/probe-lab) inside [`sinban04/useless-engineers`](https://github.com/sinban04/useless-engineers).

- In **probe-lab** we dig deep each week into whatever we are curious about — *"Dig Deep, Stay Curious."*
- In **useless-knowledge** (this repo) we publish a curated subset of that research so others can read it too.

## Topics

Totally interest-oriented — no fixed theme. Past probe-lab subjects have ranged from wireless power transfer and GPU / computer architecture to anti-cancer medication, ATP energy & kinases, AI compilers, and the principles of taste. Anything that one of us finds worth digging into is fair game.

## Reports

In reverse chronological order (newest first).

- **[2026-05-15 — Principles of Taste](report/2026-05-15-principles-of-taste-Suhwan)** *(Suhwan)*
  Korean-language seminar (~1–1.5 hours) on taste from molecule to perception: papillae and taste-bud anatomy, the five tastes (sweet / salty / sour / bitter / umami), what makes food "delicious", 9 self-experiments, and 5 appendices. Includes images and slides.

- **[2026-05-01 — ATP, Energy, and Kinases](report/2026-05-01-atp-energy-kinase)**
  Deep dive on ATP as both energy currency and signaling substrate, the ~518-member kinase family, phosphorylation cascades (PKA / MAPK / PI3K-Akt), and kinase inhibitor drugs (imatinib, EGFR-TKIs).

- **[2026-04-17 — AI Compiler](report/2026-04-17-AI-Compiler-Suhwan)** *(Suhwan)*
  AI-compiler seminar (~20–30 min, for readers with compiler basics): traditional compilers → AI compilers, MLIR, surveys of XLA / torch.compile / TVM / Triton / IREE, a torch.compile deep dive, and 4 MLIR appendices.

- **[2026-03-20 — Anti-Cancer Medication Generations](report/2026-03-20-anti-cancer-medication)**
  Multi-part research on anti-cancer medication generations (chemotherapy → targeted therapy → immunotherapy → cell therapies → mRNA), comparative analysis, future directions, and 5 glossaries.

- **[2026-03-06 — Wireless Power Transfer](report/2026-03-06-Wireless-Power-Transfer-Suhwan)** *(Suhwan)*
  Korean-language single-document seminar on wireless power transfer: electromagnetic-induction fundamentals (Faraday / Ampere / Maxwell), magnetic-flux-based power transfer, applied designs.
