## Classic And Historical Sources

- Singer SJ, Nicolson GL. [The fluid mosaic model of the structure of cell membranes](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/4333397/). *Science.* 1972;175(4023):720-731. Used for the classic membrane model reference.
- Sagan L. [On the origin of mitosing cells](https://doi.org/10.1016/0022-5193%2867%2990079-3). *Journal of Theoretical Biology.* 1967;14(3):255-274. Used as the classic serial endosymbiosis reference associated with Lynn Margulis.
- Martin W, Mentel M. [The origin of mitochondria](https://www.nature.com/articles/nature09458). *Nature.* 2010;466:920-922. Used as a modern review pointer on mitochondrial origin.
- Quirós PM, Mottis A, Auwerx J. [Mitonuclear communication in homeostasis and stress](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/26956194/). *Nature Reviews Molecular Cell Biology.* 2016;17:213-226. Used for mitochondria-to-nucleus retrograde signaling, mitonuclear coordination, and stress-response framing.
- West AP, Shadel GS. [Mitochondrial DNA in innate immune responses and inflammatory pathology](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/30079374/). *Nature Reviews Immunology.* 2017;17:363-375. Used for damaged-mitochondria mtDNA-release and innate immune signaling caveat.
- NobelPrize.org. [The 2016 Nobel Prize in Physiology or Medicine - Advanced Information](https://www.nobelprize.org/prizes/medicine/2016/advanced-information/). Used for autophagy background and the autophagosome-to-lysosome recycling framing.
- Pickles S, Vigie P, Youle RJ. [Mitophagy and Quality Control Mechanisms in Mitochondrial Maintenance](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/30699315/). *Current Biology.* 2018;28(4):R170-R185. Used for mitophagy and mitochondrial quality-control framing.
- Banani SF, Lee HO, Hyman AA, Rosen MK. [Biomolecular condensates: organizers of cellular biochemistry](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/28225081/). *Nature Reviews Molecular Cell Biology.* 2017;18:285-298. Used for membraneless organelles and phase-separation framing.
- Ginhoux F, Guilliams M. [Tissue-Resident Macrophage Ontogeny and Homeostasis](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/26441990/). *Immunity.* 2016;44(3):439-449. Used for monocyte-derived versus embryonically seeded tissue-resident macrophage origin.
